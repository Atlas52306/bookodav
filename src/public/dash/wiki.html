Instructions

Step-by-step guide for using BOOKO-DAV...

This is a storage space designed primarily to transfer ebooks to ereaders like KOReader that support webdav interface. The tech used here is R2 storage from Cloudflare and Workers from Cloudflare. While R2 and Workers aren't strictly free of cost, Cloudflare gives a very generous free tier. The pricing is explained in the Pricing section.

Pricing

BOOKO-DAV uses Cloudflare R2 and Workers, which offer a generous free tier and low costs for additional usage. Here's a breakdown of the pricing:

Feature Free Tier Additional Usage
Storage 10 GB $0.015 per GB/month
Uploads 1 million uploads/month $0.36 per million uploads
Downloads 10 million downloads/month $0.09 per million downloads
Data Transfer (Downloading Files) Unlimited (free) Free
App Requests 100,000 requests/day $0.15 per million requests

What do these terms mean?

  • Uploads: Every time you upload a file (e.g., a book or document), it counts as one upload. The free tier allows up to 1 million uploads per month.
  • Downloads: Every time you download a file, it counts as one download. The free tier allows up to 10 million downloads per month. (Note: This counts the number of times you download a file, not the size of the file.)
  • Data Transfer (Downloading Files): The amount of data you download (e.g., the total size of all files) is completely free, no matter how much you use. (Note: This refers to the total data downloaded, not the number of downloads.)
  • App Requests: Every time the app checks for files or performs an action (e.g., listing files), it counts as one request. The free tier allows up to 100,000 requests per day.

Example Costs:

  • Storing 100 GB: $1.50/month.
  • Uploading 10,000 files: $0.0036/month.
  • Downloading 10,000 files: $0.0009/month.

For most users, the free tier is more than enough. You can monitor your usage in the Cloudflare Dashboard.
